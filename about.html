A Sanctuary Rooted In Stewardship

Rumah Daisy Cantik is located in the quiet village of Kaba-Kaba, Tabanan – a window into "Old Bali" where rice terraces, ceremony rhythms, and community life remain vibrant.

Guided by a philosophy of stewardship, we treat this land as borrowed from future generations. Every stay helps sustain local culture, empower village talent, and preserve a gentle ecological balance.

From hosting humanitarian tours to welcoming solo travelers seeking renewal, our estate functions as both retreat and responsible hospitality platform.
