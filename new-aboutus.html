Who We Are

A Sanctuary Built on
Stewardship.

Located in the serene village of Kaba-Kaba, Rumah Daisy Cantik is not just a boutique hotel; it is a philosophy in practice. Managed by Mary Swanson, our estate operates on the belief that we are merely stewards of this beautiful land.

Unlike conventional resorts, we are deeply woven into the local fabric of Tabanan. Whether hosting the Gesundheit Humanitarian Clowning Tour or providing a quiet "home away from home" for solo travelers, every stay supports our commitment to community and cultural preservation.

From hosting the Gesundheit Humanitarian Clowning Tour to supporting rice field caretakers and village artisans, our initiatives align hospitality with social contribution.

  • • Ingredient sourcing from nearby growers—fresh fruit, heritage rice, village produce.
